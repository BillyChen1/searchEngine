business confidence dips in japan

business confidence among japanese manufacturers has weakened for the first time since march 2003 the quarterly tankan survey has found

slower economic growth rising oil prices a stronger yen and weaker exports were blamed for the fall decembers confidence level was below that seen in september the bank of japan said however septembers reading was the strongest for 13 years the economy is at a pause but unlikely to fall the economy minister said it will feel a bit slower next year than this year and growth may be a bit more gentle but the situation is that the recovery will continue said economy minister heizo takenaka in the bank of japans december survey the balance of big manufacturers saying business conditions are better minus those saying they are worse was 22 down from 26 in september
