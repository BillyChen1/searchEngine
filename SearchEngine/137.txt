bank set to leave rates on hold

uk interest rates are set to remain on hold at 475% following the latest meeting of the bank of england

the banks ratesetting committee has put up rates five times in the past year but rates have been on hold since september amid signs of a slowdown economic growth slowed in the previous quarter as manufacturing output fell while consumer confidence has slipped there is also growing evidence that the previously booming uk housing market is now cooling
